FAQ

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. Follow the instructions provided to reset your password.

Can I change my username?

Yes, you can change your username by going to the account settings page. Look for the option to edit your username and follow the prompts to make the changes.
